Metal Bed – Durable Elegance

This metal bed frame is crafted from powder-coated steel, providing exceptional longevity for up to 11 years under normal use. Its design emphasizes easy assembly, with over 85% repairability—so if needed, you can replace individual beams or brackets to extend its lifespan.

Packaging Details

All components ship in durable boxes made from FSC-certified cardboard. Metal parts come wrapped to avoid scratches. Single sizes often fit in one main box plus a headboard box, while double/king sizes may ship in two or three boxes total.

Size Type Box 1 Contents Box 2 Contents Headboard Box
Single 4× side beams, 2× rolled slats, 3× beam legs, center beams, cross beam, hardware pouch N/A (generally not needed for single sizes) Headboard pieces (if purchased), pillow (1×), metal plates, hardware pouch
Double / King 4× side beams, 2× rolled slats, 2× beam legs, hardware pouch Additional slats, middle beams, center beams, crossbeam, top/bottom beams, support post Headboard pieces (if purchased), pillows (2×), metal plates, hardware pouch

Quality Requirements

  • Wood Surfaces: Must be well sanded; no cracks, bark, or mold. No large knots on visible edges.
  • Metal Welding: Smooth, even seams with no sharp edges. No burrs or indentation in the tubes.
  • Labels & Stickers: “Made in China” or origin labels on the lower beam, plus recycling icons where applicable.

By following these guidelines and replacing parts as needed, you can keep your metal bed functional and stylish for years to come.
