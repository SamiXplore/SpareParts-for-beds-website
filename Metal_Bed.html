Metal Bed – Technical Overview

This metal bed frame is crafted from powder-coated steel, providing exceptional structural integrity for up to 11 years under normal use. Its design emphasizes easy assembly, with over 85% repairability, so most parts (like slats, beams, or brackets) can be replaced individually if needed.

Key Specifications

  • Steel Frame: 0.8 mm thick steel pipes with protective plastic caps on the underside. Welds are miter cut to ensure smooth corners.
  • Birch Slats: 12 mm thick, 6 cm wide, connected via hook-side Velcro strips. The 3rd slat in each set has nano tape for extra stability.
  • Height: Frame height is approximately 25 cm, ensuring clearance underneath while keeping a low profile.
  • Hardware: Assembled with M6 screws. Single sizes typically use around 12× M6×12 mm, 5× M6×37 mm, etc.; larger sizes may have extra components or screws.
  • Optional Headboard & Pillow: Headboard pieces attach with M6 screws, matching the 0.8 mm steel thickness. Light grey pillows (70×40×10 cm) can be fastened with Velcro.

Packaging Details

All components ship in durable boxes made from FSC-certified cardboard. Metal parts come wrapped to avoid scratches. Single sizes often fit in one main box plus a headboard box, while double/king sizes may ship in two or three boxes total.

Quality Requirements

  • Wood Surfaces: Must be well sanded; no cracks, bark, or mold. No large knots or brand markings on visible edges.
  • Metal Welding: Smooth, even seams with no sharp edges. No burrs or indentation in the tubes.
  • Labels & Stickers: “Made in China” or other origin labels on the lower beam, plus recycling icons per local regulations.

By following these guidelines and replacing parts as needed, you can keep your metal bed functional and aesthetically pleasing for years to come.
